p5.play Game Jam! 2022

The p5.play Game Jam is a competition held bi-annually to see who can make the best game using p5.play!

You can work individually or in a team. Share your game in the #p5play-game-jam-2022 channel on Discord to receive feedback that can help you develop and polish your game before you submit it.

Games can be submitted from December 1st to the deadline on December 16th.

The 2022 jam's theme is: Lines!

The winners will be announced in January 2023!
