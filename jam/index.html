p5play Game Jam! Spring 2023

The p5play Game Jam is a competition held tri-annually to see who can make the best game using p5play!

You can work individually or in a team. Submit your game using the google form linked below or share your game in the #share channel on Discord to receive feedback before the competition ends!

For this Jam, games that make creative use of p5play's physics engine will be more likely to win!

Games must use the latest version of p5play and be submitted before May 31st 2023.
