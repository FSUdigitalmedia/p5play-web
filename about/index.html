About

p5play (v3) was created by Quinton Ashley.

p5play is an accessible and powerful platform that inspires students to create games and art with code!

If you want more info about why I made p5play (v3), read the "p5play Version 3" wiki article and my developer log.

If you'd like to see some demos of games and art I've made with p5play, check out my OpenProcessing page.
